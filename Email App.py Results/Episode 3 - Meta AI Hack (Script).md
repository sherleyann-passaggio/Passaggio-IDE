# Episode 3: Resonance — Meta AI Hack (15-Second Draft Script)

## Production Brief
*   **App:** Resonance
*   **Angle:** Meta AI Social Engineering / Account Hijacking
*   **Framework:** Pain (Fear) + Solution (Safety)
*   **Target Duration:** 15 seconds
*   **Visual Style:** High-contrast split-screen, fast cuts, #D81B60 accents, Oswald + Lato typography.

---

## SCRIPT

### [0.00 - 3.00s] THE HOOK (Fear / Intrigue)
*Visual:*
*   Screen is dark. A glowing, sinister chatbot icon appears in the center.
*   Rapid glitch effects distort the screen. #D81B60 flashes.
*   A lock icon in the center violently shatters into digital shards.

*On-screen Text:*
*   "META AI HACKED"
*   (Typography: Oswald Bold, flashing white/red)

*Audio (Voiceover - F5-TTS-MLX):*
> "Hackers just tricked Meta's AI into handing over private accounts..."

---

### [3.00 - 6.00s] THE REVEAL (Consequence)
*Visual:*
*   Split-screen: Left shows a panicked person staring at a phone notification: "PASSWORD CHANGED. ACCESS DENIED."
*   Right side shows a cartoon hacker whispering into a sleek AI chatbot interface, which responds with a green checkmark.
*   A massive red warning sign slams over the screen.

*On-screen Text:*
*   "YOUR ACCOUNT NEXT?"

*Audio (Voiceover):*
> "...and yours might be the next target."

---

### [6.00 - 10.00s] THE PAIN (Data / Shock)
*Visual:*
*   Fast montage of news headlines and headlines: "AI TRICKED," "ACCOUNT STOLEN," "DIGITAL TRUST CRISIS."
*   Animated graph showing a sharp spike in account takeovers.
*   #D81B60 graph lines on a white background.

*On-screen Text:*
*   "DIGITAL TRUST IS DEAD"

*Audio (Voiceover):*
> "One breach can destroy your identity, your business, and your peace of mind."

---

### [10.00 - 15.00s] THE SOLUTION (Resonance)
*Visual:*
*   Transition to the Resonance App logo (clean, safe, bright).
*   Phone screen shows the Resonance interface: a liveness check (face scan) passing, a 10-minute virtual meet timer starting, and a "Verified Human" badge.
*   App Store / Play Store download badges appear at the bottom.

*On-screen Text:*
*   "RESONANCE: VERIFIED. NOT VULNERABLE."
*   "GET EARLY ACCESS" [CTA Button]

*Audio (Voiceover):*
> "Stop reacting. Start verifying. Download Resonance today and take back control of your digital identity."

---

## PRODUCTION NOTES

*   **Music:** Build tension with low, pulsing synth bass in the "Hook" and "Reveal." Transition to a clean, confident, and slightly heroic tech-sound in the "Solution" phase.
*   **Pacing:** The first 6 seconds must feel chaotic, fast, and anxiety-inducing. The transition to the Resonance App screen at 10 seconds must feel like a breath of fresh air—calm, clean, and safe.
*   **Captioning:** Keep text minimal. Oswald for impact, Lato for data points. Ensure high contrast for mobile viewing (black text on white, or white text on black with #D81B60 accents).
