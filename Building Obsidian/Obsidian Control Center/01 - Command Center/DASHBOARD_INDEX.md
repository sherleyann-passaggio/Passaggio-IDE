## 🔄 Daily Ritual

1. Open this index.
2. Scan `⚠️ RISK_AND_BLOCKERS_DASHBOARD` first.
3. Move to `📊 OPERATIONS_DASHBOARD` for today's actions.
4. Review `🏥 ECOSYSTEM_HEALTH_DASHBOARD` for any app that needs attention.
5. Check `💰 REVENUE_DASHBOARD` for pipeline changes.
6. Plan content from `🎬 CONTENT_DASHBOARD` and `🎯 STRATEGY_DASHBOARD`.
