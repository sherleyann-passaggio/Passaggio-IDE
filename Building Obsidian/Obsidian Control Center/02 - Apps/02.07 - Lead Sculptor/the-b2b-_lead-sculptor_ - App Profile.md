## 🎯 Primary Pain Point

"Stop paying influencers with fake followers"

**Kindergarten Translation:** Brands spend $10K on a campaign and discover 70% of the audience is bots. No engagement, no sales, no recourse.

---

## 🏗️ Architecture Decisions

- **Privacy-First:** `privacyMode: private` — protects brand data and creator lists.
- **Authenticity Score:** Multi-platform analysis (follower ratio, engagement velocity, comment sentiment).
- **Campaign Builder:** Drag-and-drop creator selection with ROI forecasting.
- **UGC Outreach:** Automated pitch sequences with response tracking.
