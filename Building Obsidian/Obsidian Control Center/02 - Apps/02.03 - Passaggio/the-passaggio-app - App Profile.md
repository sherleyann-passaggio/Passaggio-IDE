## 🎯 Primary Pain Point

"The silent career-killer for singers"

**Kindergarten Translation:** Vocalists lose their voice from bad habits and have no daily training plan — just generic advice from random blogs.

---

## 🏗️ Architecture Decisions

- **Microphone Access:** `microphone` frame permission for real-time vocal analysis and exercise feedback.
- **Exercise Player:** Guided warmups with tempo and pitch tracking.
- **Vocal Health Dashboard:** Daily fatigue scoring, hydration reminders, and scheduling.
- **Stripe:** Subscription tiers for advanced exercises and 1:1 coaching integration.
