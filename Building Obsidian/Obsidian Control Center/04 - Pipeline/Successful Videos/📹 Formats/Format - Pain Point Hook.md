# 📹 Format — Pain Point Hook

## Description
Opens with a sharp, relatable pain point in the first 3 seconds. The hook is designed to stop the scroll by mirroring the viewer's exact frustration.

## Best For
- Reddit-sourced pain points
- SaaS contrast narratives
- Service trades and ops frustrations

## Structure
- **0-3s:** State the pain point as a question or bold claim.
- **3-11s:** Show the consequence or the broken workflow.
- **11-15s:** Introduce the Passaggio solution with a strong CTA.

## Example Hook Lines
- "Still tracking background checks manually?"
- "Rate-limit hell is burning your credits."
- "Your outbound calls are getting screened by bots."

## Performance Notes
- High retention when the pain point is specific and recent.
- Works best when paired with a visual mockup of the broken process.
