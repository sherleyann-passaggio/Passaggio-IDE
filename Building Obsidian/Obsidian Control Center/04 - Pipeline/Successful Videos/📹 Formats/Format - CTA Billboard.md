# 📹 Format — CTA Billboard

## Description
Ends with a bold, high-contrast CTA screen that dominates the final frames. Designed to drive a single, clear action after the value has been established.

## Best For
- Lead generation
- Newsletter or waitlist signups
- Direct response campaigns

## Structure
- **0-3s:** Hook with the problem or promise.
- **3-11s:** Deliver proof or a quick demo.
- **11-15s:** Full-screen CTA billboard with one action.

## Example CTA Lines
- "Get your custom system. Link in bio."
- "Book a 15-minute diagnostic."
- "Stop stacking SaaS. Start building yours."

## Performance Notes
- Keep the CTA text under 6 words.
- Use the brand pink (`#D81B60`) on white or black for maximum contrast.
